I have kept the name and looking to turn it into a brand that, for those of us that are different, can relate to be a Str8Rogue individual.

or just hard headed. But some of us have a way of doing things that we enjoy and allows us the best way to learn.

a website, I always have to figure out how to increase efficiency on them.
